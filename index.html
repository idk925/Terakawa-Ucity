Our Craft

Our chef's recipes originate from the Kumamoto region of Japan. The broth is simmered for a period of 2 days, extracting deep, complex flavors from Natural Heritage Berkshire pork bones.

We offer two styles of fresh handmade noodles — thick wavy egg noodles for traditional ramen, and thin straight Kyushu-style noodles — both cooked al dente.

The Experience
1Taste the broth — savor the depth of 48 hours of simmering.
2Taste the noodles — appreciate the handmade texture and al dente bite.
3Experience the harmony of flavors and textures together.
4Season with table spices — a Kumamoto specialty tradition.
5Watch the flavor of your broth evolve throughout the meal.
